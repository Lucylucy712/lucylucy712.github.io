About Me

I am now a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistical Science, Southern Methodist University, advised by Dr. Xiaowei Zhan, an Associate Professor in the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center. Besides, I am also working as a student Research Assistant at Quantitative Biomedical Research Center (QBRC) at UT Southwestern
Before that, I received my Master's degree in Statistical Science from Wake Forest University(WFU) in May 2018, and a Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Wuhan University (WHU) in Jun 2016.
